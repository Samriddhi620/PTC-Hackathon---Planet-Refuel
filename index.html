Our Company

Planet Refuel is a non-profit organization that serves to communicate between food service businesses and homeless shelters to prevent food waste.

Mission

Our mission is to reduce food wastage as much as possible while also making a positive impact on society.

Vision

We want to expand to as many cities as possible to provide for those in need. We also plan on obtaining sponsorships and partnerships with other bigger companies and franchises to assist with our mission.

Values

We value honest and fulfilled work. Here at planet refuel we advocate for honest and sincere work, as we are not only doing this for ourselves but also for the greater good.
